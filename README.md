## Features

- **Product Selection**: Choose between OCC 11 or Tissue Paper
- **Pre-configured Targeting**: Industry, location, and role parameters
- **Lead Generation**: Mock lead generation (ready for API integration)
- **Results Display**: Company, contact, email, phone details
- **Export Options**: Google Sheets export and email campaign triggers

## Products

### OCC 11 (Old Corrugated Cartons)
- Target: Kraft Paper Mills, Paper Manufacturing
- Locations: All India
- Roles: Procurement, Purchase, Supply Chain

### Tissue Paper
- Target: Hospitality, Restaurants, Offices
- Locations: North India, Central India
- Roles: Procurement, Admin, General Managers
